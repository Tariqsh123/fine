Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shipping

Is shipping free?

Yes! We offer free shipping on all orders above ₨ 5,000. For orders below ₨ 5,000, a flat shipping fee of ₨ 199 (Standard) or ₨ 299 (Express) applies based on your selected delivery option.

How long does delivery take?

Express delivery takes 2-3 business days for major cities. Standard delivery takes 4-6 business days nationwide. Remote areas may take 5-7 business days. You'll receive tracking updates throughout the process.

Do you offer Cash on Delivery?

Absolutely! Cash on Delivery (COD) is available for all orders across Pakistan. Simply pay in cash when our courier partner delivers your package. A small COD fee of ₨ 50 applies for orders under ₨ 3,000.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your order is shipped, you'll receive an SMS and email with a tracking number and link. You can track your package in real-time through our courier partner's website or by contacting our support team.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship exclusively within Pakistan. International shipping will be available soon. For special requests, please contact our support team, and we'll do our best to assist you.

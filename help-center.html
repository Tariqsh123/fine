Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to most common inquiries

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD) across Pakistan, bank transfers, Easypaisa, JazzCash, and credit/debit cards via secure gateway. For premium orders, we also offer advance bank transfer options. All payments are processed securely.

How long does delivery take?

Express delivery takes 2-3 business days for major cities (Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad). For remote areas, allow 4-6 business days. You'll receive a tracking link via SMS once shipped.

What does the 2-year warranty cover?

Our 2-year warranty covers manufacturing defects, hardware failures, and battery issues under normal use. It does NOT cover physical damage, water/liquid damage, unauthorized repairs, or accessories like chargers/cables. To claim warranty, contact our support team with invoice.

How do I return a product?

Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery for unopened, unused items in original packaging. For defective products, we offer free pickup and replacement. Please contact our concierge at +92 332 3403516 or email support@fineelectronics.pk to initiate return.

Do you provide technical support after purchase?

Absolutely! We offer free 24/7 tech support via WhatsApp and phone. Our experts can help with setup, troubleshooting, software updates, and product guidance. Just call +92 332 3403516 or message us anytime.

Do you offer bulk or corporate pricing?

Yes, we provide special discounts for bulk orders, businesses, schools, and corporate gifting. Contact our B2B team at +92 332 3403516 or email corporate@fineelectronics.pk for a customized quote.

Our support team is ready to assist you with any product inquiries or technical issues.
